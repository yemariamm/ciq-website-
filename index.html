About

The concept of a static hair type is outdated. Thus we at Curl IQ are working hard to provide personalized hair care capabilities for every female consumer around the world. While at the same time reduce the gap in communication between beauty brands and consumers. Made for people with textured hair and hair care professionals who cater to them, Curl IQ is a platform that offers recommendations, plus personalized advice on the best products and practices for their hair type. Unlike Form Beauty CurlMatch & Myavanna, Curl IQ auto-downloads up to 500 Facebook images of each user, which are then evaluated using deep-learning for curl texture, length, moisture and more.
